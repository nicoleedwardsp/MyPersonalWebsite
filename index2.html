About Me

Hey, there! My name is Nicole Edwards and I am a senior at Babson College from Guatemala City. I was raised in an English/Spanish bilingual household, and I went to a German school all of my life, which is why I speak 3 languages. I have a very bubbly personality and love meeting new people, as I believe that I can learn something from anybody I cross paths with.

I decided to study at Babson College, because I am passionate about Entrepreneurship, and that's what Babson is great at. I would like to start my own business one day, so being exposed to the best entrepeneurship education in the world taught top-notch faculty is what drove me to Babson. Addtionally, Babson has a large population of international students, which is important to me considering my multicultural background. Finally, Babson is located near the amazing city of Boston, which is a hotspot for the brightest people working in the newest developments across a wide range of industries. What I plan to do after graduation is to start a career in Consulting or Marketing. Hopefully later during my career, I can be an entrepreneur myself... and be able to travel the world!
